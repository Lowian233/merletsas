Discover Our Story

At Merlet, we combine tradition and quality to bring the best from our orchards to the international market.

Our History

Founded in 1950, Merlet began as a small family business dedicated to growing citrus fruits. Over the years, we have grown to become one of the leading citrus exporters, recognized for our dedication to quality and sustainability.

Our Values

Sustainability

We are committed to agricultural practices that respect the environment and promote a sustainable future.

Quality

Each fruit is grown to the highest standards, ensuring exceptional freshness and flavor.

Commitment

Our priority is to build lasting relationships with our customers and communities.
